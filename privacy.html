Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-12

PennyLink is committed to privacy by design. This pre-release policy outlines our approach during the beta period.

Information we collect

  • Account information: email address and basic profile details.
  • Usage data: anonymized analytics events to improve the product.
  • Waitlist: your email for product updates; unsubscribe anytime.

How we use information

  • To provide and improve PennyLink’s services.
  • To communicate important updates (beta invites, product changes).
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Limited service providers (e.g., analytics, email) may process data on our behalf under strict agreements.

Security

We use industry-standard security practices. For `.app` we require HTTPS by default.

Your choices

  • Unsubscribe from emails at any time via the link in our messages.
  • Request deletion of your data by contacting privacy@pennylink.app.

Contact

Questions? Email privacy@pennylink.app.
